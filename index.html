Who are we?
Why
should you trust us?

At our Gudauri paragliding company, we create unforgettable flights, prioritizing safety with seasoned pilots. Your well-being is our sacred commitment, fortified with unwavering dedication

STANDARD

The cost of a standard flight is 350 GEL, video recording is included, the duration of the flight is 15 minutes

LONG FLIGHT

A long flight is 30 minutes and costs 500 GEL, Also video recording and more emotions

AEROBATICS

Acro flight is for more extreme and adrenaline lovers, the duration of the flight is 15 minutes and acrobatic tricks are also added, the price is 500 GEL
